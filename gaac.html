The GITAM Aero Astro Club, founded in 2016, is a hub for students passionate about aeronautics and space science. It offers hands-on experience in designing and building aircraft models like RC planes and ornithopters, bridging theory and practice. Beyond aeromodelling, the club organizes workshops, competitions, and exciting events like the Asteroid Search Campaign, where students contribute to real-world asteroid detection. It also collaborates in technical fests, fostering innovation and teamwork across campus

MISSION

The GITAM Aero Astro Club (GAAC) is dedicated to inspiring students' interest in aeronautics and astronomy through hands-on learning experiences. By facilitating projects, workshops, and competitions, the club enhances students' practical knowledge in aerospace engineering and promotes exploration in space sciences.

VISION

GAAC envisions itself as a premier student-driven platform for aerospace and space exploration, bringing together future engineers and innovators. The club aims to create a collaborative environment where members can work on advanced projects, contributing significantly to the fields of aeronautics and astronomy.

VALUES

The club upholds key values such as innovation, encouraging creative solutions; collaboration, promoting teamwork and partnerships; practical learning, focusing on real-world applications; curiosity, driving a passion for knowledge in aerospace; and community engagement, participating in initiatives that raise awareness about aerospace sciences.
